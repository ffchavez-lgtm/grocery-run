Two rhythms: check the green WEEKLY stores for your fresh run, and the amber STOCK-UP clubs only on a Denver trip. Set the budget to match whichever trip you're doing. Bulk staples cost more up front but far less per week — that's where the savings are.

Snacks: apples, baby carrots, almonds, a square of dark chocolate, blueberries. Drinks: coffee, green tea, sparkling water, or an Olipop or Liquid Death. Roast chicken, the bulk ground beef, and a batch of beans are meant to carry across several days.
